Research Assumptions Mapping Template

A framework for systematically analyzing and validating research assumptions

Purpose

This template helps researchers map out their core assumptions, identify validation needs, and anticipate potential failure points. Use this as a foundation for building robust research methodologies while maintaining awareness of edge cases and measurement challenges.

Usage Instructions

  • Domain/Area: What aspect of your research this assumption covers (e.g., "Data Collection", "User Behavior", "System Performance")
  • Assumption Statement: The specific belief or premise you're operating from
  • Testable: Whether this assumption can be validated empirically (Yes/No/Partial)
  • Risk Level: What happens if this assumption is wrong (High/Medium/Low impact)
  • Evidence Type Needed: What kind of data or proof would validate or invalidate this assumption
  • Measurement Approach: Specific methods or tools you could use to test this assumption
  • Edge Case Vulnerability: Conditions where this assumption might break down or fail
